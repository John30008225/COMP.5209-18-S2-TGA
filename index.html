Campus Cafes


Welcome to Toi Ohomai Cafe

Yes, student life can be demanding, but luckily there is coffee all around you on campus.
Pair this with a piece of cake (or a healthier option if that’s more your thing) and you’re good to go.

There are a number of water coolers, snack, and drink machines placed conveniently around the campuses.
Also be sure to flash your coffee loyalty card at Mokoia and Windermere.

Taupo and Tokoroa have their own kitchen facilities and you'll find a canteen at Whakatane campus.
